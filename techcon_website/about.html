Our history

TechCon was founded in 2010 with the goal of bringing together technology enthusiasts, professionals, and innovators from around the world. Over the years, it has grown into one of the largest and most respected technology conferences globally.

Our mission

At TechCon, our mission is to foster innovation and collaboration in the technology sector. We aim to provide a platform for knowledge sharing, networking, and showcasing the latest advancements in technology.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Amina Yusuf

A leading voice in AI ethics, Dr. Yusuf has worked on international frameworks for responsible AI. Her keynote in 2022 explored the intersection of machine learning and social equity.

Leonardo Vega

Vega is a cybersecurity expert known for exposing vulnerabilities in global financial systems. At TechCon 2021, he delivered a powerful talk on defending against emerging cyber threats.

Mei Chen

As a pioneer in cloud architecture, Mei Chen has led cloud infrastructure transformations at top-tier tech companies. She inspired attendees in 2020 with insights into scalable cloud solutions.
